Let's Get Started

Hello. My name is Jumanai Cox and I'm a graphic designer. I was born and raised in Queens, New York City. As a child, I've always had a strong passion for illustration and design after countless hours of watching my father sit at the table and draw. This ignited my fire and thus began my destiny into the world of graphic design. It hasn't been an easy road nor is it the right path for everyone, but those of us with that "spark" find so much pleasure in the aestheticism and complexity of our work. I aspire to become the best designer I can be by learning every day and fine-tuning my craft.
